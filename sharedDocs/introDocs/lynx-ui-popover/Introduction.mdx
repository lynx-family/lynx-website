## Structure

```jsx
<PopoverRoot>
  <PopoverTrigger />
  <PopoverAnchor>
    <PopoverPositioner>
      <PopoverContent />
      <PopoverArrow />
    </PopoverPositioner>
  </PopoverAnchor>
</PopoverRoot>
```
